# Overview
The ISG serves as Box sector's Security Guidelines, or what's commonly referred to as Space Law in most Nanotransen owned sections of space. Due to the lower-activity nature of the sector, the ISG was formed by the local Central Command authority to accomodate said area of space.

Any and all documentation regarding the ISG can be accessed from this page.

>[Contraband](/rules/internal-security-guidelines/contraband) listings can be found here.
The [Crime Directory](/rules/internal-security-guidelines/crimes) serves as a list of chargeable offenses.
A [Search and Arrest Outline](/rules/internal-security-guidelines/search-and-arrest) dictates what the procedure should be for any Security on station regarding such actions.
The full [Internal Security Guidelines](/rules/internal-security-guidelines) may be read here for extra details.
