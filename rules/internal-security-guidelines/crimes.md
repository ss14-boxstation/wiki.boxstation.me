# Mutually Exclusive Crimes
Some crimes may not be charged together, either due to overlapping in their nature, or being the same crime at a separate severity. Such crimes include, grouped by their relations:
- **Theft** X-00 charges (Theft, Theft of Departmental Equipment, Theft of Weaponry, and Espionage)
- **Subversion of Justice** X-01, X-02, & X-03 Charges (Perjury, Obstruction of Justice, Forgery, Bribery, Espionage, Abuse of Power, Dereliction of Authority, Sedition)
- **Custody** X-03 & X-04 Charges (Breach of Custody, Breach of Confinement, Breach of Long-term Confinement, and Breach of Permanent Confinement).
- **Endangerment** X-05 Charges (Reckless Operation and Malpractice).
- **Trespassing** X-06 & X-07 charges (Trespassing, Breaking and Entering, Secure Trespass, Secure Breaking and Entering)
- **Bodily harm** X-08 & X-09 charges (Brawling, Assault, Manslaughter/Lethal Assault, Murder, Obstruction of Revival, Prevention of Revival, Mass Murder, Mass Prevention of Revival, Serial Murder)
- **Sabotage** X-10 & X-11 charges (Sabotage, Capital Sabotage, Irreparable Sabotage, and Broken Arrow).
- **Antisocial** X-14 to X-18 Charges (Fearmongering, Hooliganism, Vandalism, Brandishing, Vigilantism, and Brawling)
# Misdemeanours
Sentences where the highest tier crime is a Misdemeanour **cannot exceed** a total brig time of **5 minutes.**

For a first offence or minor offence Misdemeanour, **a warning** should be given first.
For repeat offence or multiple offence Misdemeanour, **up to 1 minute** can be given per crime.
- **1-00 Theft**
- **1-03 Failure to Comply**
- **1-05 Reckless Operation**
- **1-06 Trespassing**
- **1-08 Brawling**
- **1-13 Animal Cruelty**
- **1-14 Hooliganism**
- **1-15 Vandalism**
- **1-16 Fearmongering**
- **1-17 Brandishing**
- **1-18 Vigilantism**
# Felonies
Sentences where the highest tier crime is a Felony **cannot exceed** a total brig time of **10 minutes**.

For a Felony, **up to 5 minutes** can be given per crime.
- **2-00 Theft of Departmental Equipment**
- **2-01 Forgery**
- **2-02 Perjury**
- **2-03 Obstruction of Justice**
- **2-05 Malpractice**
- **2-06 Secure Trespass**
- **2-07 Breaking and Entering**
- **2-08 Manslaughter**
- **2-09 Assault**
- **2-10 Sabotage**
- **2-12 Malicious use of Substances**
- **2-14 Recidivism**
- **2-18 Black Marketeering**
# Grand Crimes
Sentences where the highest tier crime is a Grand Crime **cannot exceed** a total brig time of **20 minutes.**

For a Grand Crime, **up to 10 minutes** can be given per crime.
- **3-00 Theft of Weaponry**
- **3-02 Bribery**
- **3-06 Secure Breaking and Entering**
- **3-09 Murder**
- **3-12 Malicious use of Stimulants**
# Extreme Crimes
Sentences where the highest tier crime is an Extreme Crime **cannot exceed** a total brig time of **25 minutes.**

For an Extreme Crime, **up to 10 minutes** can be given per crime.
- **4-00 Espionage**
- **4-01 Abuse of Power**
- **4-02 Dereliction of Authority**
- **4-03 Breach of Custody**
- **4-05 Unlawful Removal of a Permanent Mindshield**
- **4-08 Obstruction of Revival**
# Capital Crimes
Sentences where the highest tier crime is a Capital Crime **cannot exceed** a total brig time of **30 minutes.**
If the sentence reaches a time **greater than 35 minutes** before the sentence cap is applied, and/or the severity of the crimes committed warrant it, a sentence of **Permanent Confinement With Appeal may be applied instead.** 

For a Capital Crime, **up to 15 minutes** can be given per crime.
- **5-01 Sedition**
- **5-03 Breach of Confinement**
- **5-08 Mass Murder**
- **5-09 Prevention of Revival**
- **5-10 Capital Sabotage**
# Crimes Against Sapience
Any Crime Against Sapience **must carry** with it the sentence of **Permanent Confinement Without Appeal.**
If **three or more** Crimes Against Sapience were committed, and no Brig Modifier Deductions or Circumstance apply, **a Trial for Execution may be warranted**.

Accessory to, Conspiracy to commit, or an Attempt of a Crime Against Sapience should be charged as a Capital Crime.
- **6-03 Breach of Long-term Confinement**
- **6-04 Breach of Permanent Confinement**
- **6-05 Non-consensual Servitorization**
- **6-08 Mass Prevention of Revival**
- **6-09 Serial Murder**
- **6-10 Irreparable Sabotage**
- **6-11 Broken Arrow**
- **6-12 Bioterrorism**
- **6-13 Cannibalism**
