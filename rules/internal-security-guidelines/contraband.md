# Contraband

Certain items and objects present a **possible or definite danger** to the crew or station, and should be handled accordingly.

Any contraband **linked to a crime should be held** in a designated Contraband Storage unit, so that NanoTrasen and Central Command can investigate further at their leisure.

The Security Department and Command Members should **never use Enemy Contraband**, and should refrain from using any contraband outside Red Alert or greater.

Permits can be granted for any Non-Enemy Contraband and a **permit should be offered first** instead of confiscating Contraband, as long as said Contraband is not linked to a crime.
Contraband Substances can only be given a permit through the means of a Legal Prescription as authorized by the Chief Medical Officer, Acting Chief Medical Officer, or Senior Chemist.

Any Contraband on an inmate should be confiscated unless they have a Permit. A Permit is considered **void** if the Contraband was **used in violation of the ISG**, such as if it was used in a crime outside of self-defence.
# Departmental Equipment
Certain items and objects are given to specific departments by NanoTrasen despite the danger or risk they present to the crew in the wrong hands because they are useful or necessary in performing the tasks given to a department.

These should not be handed outside of the department lightly, and any permits for their usage outside of the department should receive approval from the Relevant Head of Department.

Possession of Central Command Equipment and Command Equipment can be considered Espionage only if theft or intent to deceive can be proven. Even if the crime of ***Espionage*** is not committed, these should **always be confiscated** and **cannot have a Permit granted**, outside of the authorization of Central Command itself.

Any items that can be found shift-start inside the department, on departmental employees, crafted in departmental lathes, or found inside vending machines or lockers inside the department is **automatically permitted**.

# Light Contraband
Any object or item whose primary purpose is to defend or attack is considered Light Contraband. Any item capable of attacking but whose primary purpose is utility is not.
# Contraband Substances
Contraband substances are any non-medical substances. Substances intended to poison, impede, or otherwise harm members of the crew, and stimulants are considered Contraband Substances.

Intoxicant substances without further usage are primarily considered a Medical Concern and are thus the domain of the Psychologist and wider Medical Department.
The Bartender has full rights to store and supply any intoxicant and non-intoxicant Drink. Any Drink with further usage should only be supplied with informed consent.

The Science Department, as well as the Chemistry and Hydroponic Subdepartments, have automatic permits to produce and store any Contraband Substances inside their Departments.
The Hydroponic Subdepartment has an automatic permit to supply the Chemistry Subdepartment with any Contraband Substance, and any botanical produce containing any substance (including substances considered Enemy Contraband).
The Chemistry Subdepartment has an automatic permit to supply any Contraband Substance if it is to fulfill a Legal Prescription.

Vestine and its derived substances are to be treated primarily as Enemy Contraband and so are not allowed to be stored or used by any department, cannot be granted permits for storage or usage, and should be confiscated.
# Enemy Contraband
Any non-cosmetic object or item that is the Intellectual Property of an Enemy of the Corporation, such as Syndicate or Wizard objects or items, is considered Enemy Contraband.
